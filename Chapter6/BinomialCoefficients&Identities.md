### Terms

- binomial(이항식)
A binomial is a polynomial with exactly two terms.
- coefficients(계수)
A coefficient is the numerical part of a term that is multiplied by a variable. It's the number "in front of" a letter. If there is no number, the coefficient is understood to be 1.
- Expansion(전개) 
expansion means to multiply out the terms in an expression. When you expand an expression with brackets (parentheses), you are removing the brackets to write it as a sum or difference of individual terms.
- factoring(인수분해)
factoring is the process of breaking down an expression into a product of its factors. Factors are numbers or expressions that are multiplied together to get the original expression.
- trinomials(삼항식)
A trinomial is a polynomial with exactly three terms.
- polynomial (다항식)
- Symmectric (대칭의)
- theorem (정리)
A major result that has been proven to be true.
- corollary (따름 정리, 계)
A corollary is a statement that is a direct and immediate consequence of another proven statement (a theorem).
